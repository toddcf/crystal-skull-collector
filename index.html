Instructions


  1. Each crystal has a hidden value. Click on crystals to collect them.

  2. Watch your score increase with each crystal you collect.

  3. Collect enough crystals to match the maximum value without going over, and you escape with your life!

  4. But be careful -- take more crystals than the maximum value, and the walls of the cave will collapse on you!
